Frequently Asked Questions

What is Texteye?

Texteye is a text editor that you can use for editing, writing, copying, pasting, etc. The Texteye installer was made in December 5, 2022 and the website was made in December 8, 2022.

Is Texteye free?

Yes! Texteye is free to download. You don't need a credit card to buy it. It's free forever!
